Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

HIITIO Social MVP is an internal social media operations workspace used to manage original content planning, media adaptation, competitor tracking, publishing preparation, and performance reporting.

Information We Process

The app may process account names, platform handles, scheduled post information, content drafts, media metadata, asset usage records, publishing status, and analytics metrics such as views, impressions, likes, comments, shares, saves, and clicks.

TikTok and Third-Party Platform Data

If TikTok or another platform integration is enabled, the app may access only the data authorized by the user and allowed by the platform API permissions. This may include account identity, content publishing status, post metadata, and performance metrics needed for scheduling, reporting, or operational review.

How We Use Information

Information is used to operate the social media workflow, prepare original content, adapt media for platform requirements, validate publishing readiness, detect failures, generate reports, and improve scheduling and content decisions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with authorized team members, approved service providers, and third-party platforms only as needed to operate the workflow or comply with platform API requirements.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for operational, reporting, compliance, or product development purposes. API tokens or credentials should not be stored in public files, logs, or reports.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect data. Users should not enter production secrets, private access tokens, or platform credentials into public demo versions of the MVP.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project owner through the GitHub repository: hiitio-social-mvp-site.
